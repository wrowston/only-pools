Only Pools is for private NFL prediction Pools. Every Pool has one Pool Season, one Pool type, one shared Pool Ruleset, and its own membership.

Use **My Pools** as your home base. It shows every Pool you belong to, your standing and pick status, and the next action available to you. Archived Pools are hidden by default; choose **Show archived** when you need one.

## Choose a Pool type

Only Pools supports two formats:

- **Survivor** — choose one NFL team each included week. A win keeps you Alive. A loss, tie, or missing required pick eliminates you. You cannot use the same team twice in one Pool.
- **Confidence** — predict the winner of every required game, then assign each game a unique confidence value. Correct picks earn their assigned value. Weekly and Season Standings track the results.

The Pool type cannot change after creation. If you are deciding which one to run, choose Survivor for one consequential choice each week and Confidence for a full Pick Sheet with weekly point totals.

<GuideCallout>
Only Pools does not enroll anyone just because they opened an invite. Each person signs in, reviews the Pool and privacy disclosure, and explicitly accepts.
</GuideCallout>

## If you run a Pool

1. Open **My Pools** and choose **Create Pool**.
2. Select Survivor or Confidence, a Start Week, and a Pick Lock mode.
3. Open the Pool’s **Pool** section to create and share its Pool Invite.
4. Use the same section to manage Members, Admins, ownership, archival, and the Pool Audit.
5. Make your own picks from **Board** just like every other Participant.

Start with [Create a Pool and Reuse a Template](/guides/create-a-pool), then read [Invite People and Join a Pool](/guides/invites-and-joining).

## If you play in a Pool

1. Open the Pool Invite supplied by its Owner or Admin.
2. Sign in with a verified email address and phone number.
3. Review the contact-visibility disclosure and choose **Accept invite**.
4. Open **Board** and make your pick. Changes save automatically.
5. Open **Standings** to follow official results and ranks.

Read [Use the Week Board](/guides/week-board-picks-and-locks), then continue with [Make Survivor Picks](/guides/survivor-picks) or [Make Confidence Picks](/guides/confidence-picks).

## Find your way around

Inside a Pool, navigation has three sections:

- **Board** is where you move between available Pool Weeks and make picks.
- **Standings** shows official weekly or season results appropriate to the Pool type.
- **Pool** shows Members, roles, invitations, administrative actions, reports, and audit events.

On a smaller screen these appear as navigation chips. On a larger screen they appear in the Pool sidebar. **Help** returns you to these guides without changing anything in your Pool.

## Important terms

Only Pools uses **Pool Owner**, **Pool Admin**, and **Pool Member** for roles. Everyone competing is a **Participant**. A **Pick Lock** is the irreversible point after which a pick cannot be submitted or changed. A **Verified Result** is an official result that can affect standings; a projected result is informational only.

The [Pool Rules and Lifecycle reference](/guides/pool-rules-and-lifecycle) explains the complete user-facing vocabulary.
