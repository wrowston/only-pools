A Pool Invite is a shareable credential for joining one Active Pool. Ordinary invites can be used by more than one eligible person. Returning Participant Invites are person-specific and single-use.

## Create or retrieve an invite

Creating a Pool mints an ordinary invite immediately and shows a copyable share link on the success screen.

Later, Pool Owners and Pool Admins can retrieve or manage that invite while admission is open:

1. Open the Pool.
2. Choose **Pool**.
3. Under **Pool Invite**, choose **Create / retrieve invite**.
4. Complete Step-up Verification when prompted.
5. Choose **Copy link** and send the full link privately to the people you want to invite.

Only one ordinary Pool Invite is active at a time. It expires after the displayed time. Retrieving the active invite does not create a second credential.

## Rotate an invite

Choose **Rotate invite** when the current link was shared too broadly or should no longer be accepted. Rotation invalidates the previous link immediately and creates a replacement. Send the replacement only to the intended people.

Do not paste an invite into an Abuse Report, public post, analytics field, or other place where unintended people could receive it.

## Accept an invite

Opening an invite does not enroll you.

1. Open the complete invite link.
2. Sign in with an account that has a verified email address and phone number.
3. Review the Pool name, type, and Start Week.
4. Read and select the contact-visibility acknowledgment.
5. Choose **Accept invite**.

After acceptance, Only Pools opens the Week Board. If you already belong to the Pool, choose **Open Week Board** instead.

<GuideCallout>
The Pool Owner and Pool Admins can see a current Participant’s verified email address and phone number for Pool administration. The acceptance screen discloses this before you join.
</GuideCallout>

## Returning Participant Invites

A Returning Participant Invite comes from a Pool created with a prior-season template. It is tied to the invited Participant and may propose either Pool Member or Pool Admin.

Open the person-specific link, sign in with the invited account, review the proposed role and privacy disclosure, and choose **Accept invite** or **Accept as Pool Admin**. A different account cannot claim the invitation.

## When admission closes

An ordinary or Returning Participant Invite cannot be accepted when:

- the earliest Start Week game has reached its scheduled kickoff;
- the Pool is Completed or Archived;
- the invite expired, was rotated, or was already used when person-specific; or
- membership admission is otherwise closed.

A later schedule change does not reopen admission after the cutoff has passed.

## If an invite is unavailable

Ask the Pool Owner or Admin to check the link in the Pool’s **Pool Invite** section. They may need to retrieve the current invite or rotate it and send the replacement. Confirm that you opened the full URL and signed in with the correct account.
