# Choose the SportsDataIO production contract

## Question

Which SportsDataIO product/feed and commercial terms should the production MVP adopt, and what operator-funded budget, capacity, licensing, SLA, and fallback assumptions must the specification enforce while the app remains free to every participant?

## Answer

Ruled out of scope after [Obtain SportsDataIO production proposals](./14-obtain-sportsdataio-production-proposals.md) established a $600 annual production price that the project owner rejected as too expensive for the free MVP. No SportsDataIO product will be selected in this effort; provider selection continues in [Select the production NFL data provider](./16-select-production-nfl-data-provider.md).
